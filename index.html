a word about us

Hi, my name is kelly and i'm the owner and operator of freckles and curls sweets. so glad you are here! While it's true that i didn't grow up loving to bake, was taught at a young age how to prepare food,and loved seeing the transformation and the process involved. from a few simple ingredients, to a creation made with love. the combination of the different flavors intrigued me!

I’ve always had a creative side, and started pursuing it in 2019. I enrolled at the Escoffier Culinary School and earned my Pastry Arts Certificate with high honors! You can know that when I'm working on your exquisite treat that not only is being made with passion, and love, but understanding of all the ingredients and it’s methodological process!

Freckles And Curls Sweets is located in Charlotte, North Carolina, and serves its surrounding areas. I was born in Colombia, South America. Naturally, I bring the Latin flavor and love to infuse my culture in the desserts I create! I live with my husband and our three wonderful children who keep us busy! Aside from making delicious treats, I'm a Stay At Home Mom, and love to serve my community through my local church. Thank you so much for considering my small business! I appreciate your support, and I look forward to connecting with you and creating a delectable and custom treat just for you, and/or to celebrate your special occasion!
